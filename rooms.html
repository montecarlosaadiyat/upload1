Family Suite

There's no shortage of space in our Family Suites which offer flexible living in a contemporary and relaxing space. Decorated with warm Moroccan tones our one-bedroom Family Suites are located on the Executive floor making them suitable for long staying guest, executive travellers and families. Family Suites offer 55-70m sqm of living space, a 40-inch HD LCD TV in the bedroom as well as the living room, a seating area, a writing desk, a minibar and air-conditioning. All rooms feature a king-size bed, complimentary tea and coffee as well as a hairdryer and iron. A luxurious bathroom includes both a bathtub and eco-friendly walk-in shower. Complimentary high speed internet, breakfast, afternoon tea, evening cocktails are also available from the Executive Lounge. Family Suites can accommodate 2 adults and 1 child or a maximum of 3 persons with a chargeable extra bed subject to availability. Interconnecting rooms, smoking or non smoking options are available on request.

Luxury Double Suite

Designed to reflect nine of the countries adventurer Ibn Battuta visited, the themed Battuta one-bedroom Suites of 85 sqm are located on each floor of the hotel. They offer a king-size bed, a living room as well as a spacious terrace with outdoor loungers and views on the city. The luxurious Suites include a 40-inch, HD LCD TV in both bedrooms and living room, writing desk with iPod dock and DVD player, a minibar and air-conditioning. There is a large bathroom with separate bathtub and shower room which features an eco-friendly rain shower and upgraded amenities. An extra bathroom is provided for your guests. Access to the Executive Lounge facilities is complimentary and includes breakfast, afternoon tea, evening cocktails, tea and coffee. Battuta suites can accommodate 2 adults and 1 child or a maximum of 3 persons with a chargeable extra bed subject to availability. Interconnecting rooms, smoking or non smoking options are available on request.
